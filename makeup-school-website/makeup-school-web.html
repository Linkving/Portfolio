Teachers

Meet Iha

Makeup artist

My love affair with makeup began when I was an account executive for an advertising agency in New York City.

After getting training and certification, I worked
as a freelance Makeup Artist. I have worked with many talented photographers, and agency represented models such as Wilhelmina, MC2 Miami, Elite Model Management, Ford Models, DAS models, the Syndical, and NEXT.

In 2014 I worked at NY Fashion week for the first time
and since than I did not miss a single show. I joined A.C.E.
in 2018.

Meet Sheela

Makeup and hair artist

My love for art and makeup started at a very early age, and in 2010 this passion blossomed into a career.

After attending cosmetology school, I dabbled in hair design, but my true love resided in makeup. I spent
over 4 years working for MAC Cosmetics. I completed
a rigorous, comprehensive certification makeup-artistry training program and continued to acquire education
on current makeup trends, techniques, and latest
product knowledge.

In 2016, I left my position at MAC and joined A.C.E.
in London.

Meet Robert

Special effects makeup artist

I have always been passionate about art and one
of the most exquisite expressions of art - makeup.

Trained at London College of Fashion, I am experienced in working in a variety of fields across fashion, commercials, advertising, corporate,
music promos and television.

I have regularly worked backstage at London Fashion Week and London Men’s Collections. I have also been Make-up Designer & Lead Make-up Artist of collections at the Mercedes-Benz Miami
Fashion Week.
